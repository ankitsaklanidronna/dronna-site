Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Dronna collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use dronna.in and related services.

Effective Date: March 28, 2026 | Last Updated: March 28, 2026
We collect account, usage, and payment-related confirmation data only to run the platform, improve learning experience, and support secure transactions.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and password at signup.
  • Usage data: pages visited, attempts, scores, and learning progress.
  • Device and log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and referral URLs.
  • Payment data: payments are processed through Razorpay. We do not store card, UPI PIN, or CVV details on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your Dronna account.
  • To provide access to question banks, practice sets, and mock tests.
  • To track progress and personalize your learning experience.
  • To process payment confirmations and provide subscription access.
  • To send important service updates, notices, and policy changes.
  • To monitor fraud, misuse, and platform security.

3. Razorpay Payment Processing

Dronna uses Razorpay for online payments. All sensitive payment information is collected and processed on Razorpay's secure infrastructure. We only receive limited transaction confirmation details required to activate or verify purchases.

By making a payment on Dronna, you also agree to Razorpay's Privacy Policy.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Service providers such as Razorpay and Supabase.
  • Analytics or infrastructure tools used to improve platform quality.
  • Authorities where required by law or valid legal process.
  • Security partners if needed to prevent fraud or misuse.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as needed to operate your account, provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain records related to transactions.

6. Cookies and Sessions

Dronna may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember settings, and understand usage patterns. Disabling them may affect some features.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards including HTTPS, access controls, and secure backend services. No online system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

  • You may request access to your personal data.
  • You may request correction of inaccurate information.
  • You may request account and data deletion, subject to legal or operational retention needs.
  • You may opt out of non-essential communications.
For privacy-related requests, account deletion, or payment concerns, contact us at support@dronna.in.

9. Children's Privacy

Dronna is intended for users aged 13 years and above. Users under 18 should use the platform with parent or guardian guidance.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Dronna after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.
