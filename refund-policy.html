Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy explains when refunds may be issued for Dronna subscription purchases and how cancellation or duplicate-payment issues are handled.

Effective Date: March 28, 2026 | Last Updated: March 28, 2026
We aim to keep refunds clear and fair while protecting the platform from misuse and excessive content consumption.

1. Eligibility for Refund

You may request a refund within 7 days of purchase if:

  • you were charged more than once for the same transaction;
  • payment was successful but access was not granted due to a technical issue;
  • you reported a major platform-access issue within the refund window; or
  • you have not consumed a significant portion of the paid content.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

  • refund request made after 7 days of purchase;
  • extensive usage or substantial consumption of paid content;
  • dissatisfaction based only on exam results or personal outcomes;
  • change of mind after using the service; or
  • account suspension due to misuse or violation of Terms.

3. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel future renewals at any time. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically create a refund for the remaining period already paid for.

4. Duplicate or Failed Payments

If you were charged twice, or payment was deducted but premium access was not activated, please contact us with your payment reference or Razorpay Payment ID. Genuine duplicate-payment cases will be resolved as quickly as possible.

5. How to Request a Refund

Please email support@dronna.in with:

  • your registered Dronna email address;
  • Razorpay Payment ID or transaction reference;
  • date of purchase; and
  • reason for refund request.

6. Refund Method

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the transaction. We do not issue cash refunds or transfer refunds to unrelated accounts.

For payment disputes or duplicate charges, contact us before raising a chargeback so we can help resolve the issue faster.
